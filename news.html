Ilsan Lake Park

Ilsan Lake Park is the largest artificial lake park. Designed to provide a natural-looking space for outdoor recreation in an urban area that is isolated from natural water and trees. There are over 100 different kinds of wild flowers, a forest with more than 200,000 trees, and a healthy population of red-crowned cranes.
Its wide range of facilities include a waterfront square, an artificial island, about 5-km connecting bike path, a parking lot, three playgrounds, a nature experience center, and a music fountain. The lake park hosts Goyang Korea Flower Show and other fairs and exhibitions.


How to get to Ilsan Lake Park:
Address: 906, Janghang-dong, Ilsandong-gu,
Goyang-si (경기 고양시 일산동구 595)
To get there by subway Jeongbalsan station (정발산역)
